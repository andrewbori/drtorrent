Mobclix for Android 4.1.0 Documentation

Welcome to the Mobclix Developer Program!

Thank you for taking the time to check out the Mobclix SDK for Android. The Mobclix SDK for Android enables your application to access the Mobclix Mobile Platform - a powerful developer utility. Consisting of the enterprise Mobclix Advertising Exchange and Mobclix Analytics, the Mobclix Mobile Platform gives you both lucrative methods of making money from your applications and valuable insights into the usage of your applications.
